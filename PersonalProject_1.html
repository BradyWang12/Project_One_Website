Veggie Cats

Project Description

Developed a 2.5D top-down farming simulator in Unity featuring time management mechanics. Players grow crops, fish, and explore the environment while managing an inventory system. The project emphasizes exploration, and resource management. Key mechanics implemented include:

  • Farming and crop management with growth cycles
  • Shop system for purchasing new items and crops
  • Debt system to pressure players to manage their time and money efficiently
  • Time management for day/night cycles
  • Inventory system for storing items, tools, and resources
  • Exploration mechanics for discovering new areas and minigames
